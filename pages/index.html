About Us

Masters Hardwood Flooring is a locally owned and operated company that specializes in installing, refinishing, and repairing hardwood floors. We have been serving the Colorado Springs area for over 12 years, delivering quality workmanship and customer satisfaction. We offer a wide range of hardwood flooring options, from solid to engineered, from prefinished to unfinished, from domestic to exotic. We can help you choose the best flooring for your home, budget, and lifestyle. We also provide free estimates, competitive pricing, and professional installation. Whether you need to install new hardwood floors, refinish your existing ones, or repair any damages, we are the experts you can trust. We have the skills, experience, and equipment to handle any hardwood flooring project, big or small. We are licensed, insured, and bonded, and we guarantee our work for one year. At Masters Hardwood Flooring, we are passionate about hardwood floors. We take pride in our work and treat every project as if it were our own. We are committed to providing you with the best hardwood flooring service in the Colorado Springs area. Contact us today to get started on your dream floors!
